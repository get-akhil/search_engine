Data Science & Analytics

1. INTRODUCTION
Data Science is the interdisciplinary field that extracts knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data using statistics, programming, and domain expertise.

2. HISTORY & EVOLUTION

1960s–1980s: Early database management and statistical analysis.

1990s: Data warehousing and Business Intelligence (BI).

2000s–Present: Big Data, machine learning, AI, and cloud analytics.
