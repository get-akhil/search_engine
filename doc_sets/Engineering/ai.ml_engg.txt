Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

1. INTRODUCTION
AI & ML are branches of computer science that enable machines to learn from data, make decisions, and solve complex problems without explicit programming.

2. HISTORY & EVOLUTION

1950s: Alan Turing’s work and the concept of machine intelligence.

1960s–1980s: Early AI programs, expert systems, and neural networks.

1990s–2000s: Machine learning algorithms and data-driven AI approaches.

2010s–Present: Deep learning, natural language processing, and generative AI.
