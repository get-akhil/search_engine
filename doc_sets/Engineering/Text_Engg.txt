Textile Engineering

1. INTRODUCTION
Textile Engineering focuses on designing, manufacturing, and developing fibers, yarns, fabrics, and textile products.
It combines principles of chemistry, mechanical engineering, and design to produce functional and aesthetic textile solutions.

2. HISTORY & EVOLUTION

Ancient Era: Hand weaving, natural fibers like cotton and silk.

Industrial Revolution: Mechanized spinning, weaving, and dyeing technologies.

20th Century: Synthetic fibers, technical textiles, and automated production.

21st Century: Smart textiles, wearable tech, and sustainable fabrics.
